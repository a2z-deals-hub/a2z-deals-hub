How to Choose Budget Headphones (Without Regrets)

A practical 5-step method to pick headphones that fit your lifestyle, without overpaying.

1) Define your use

Are you commuting, working out, or taking calls? Your use determines priorities like noise isolation, water resistance, or mic quality.

2) Pick the right form

  • In-ear: Great for the gym and portability.
  • On-ear: Compact with decent comfort.
  • Over-ear: Best comfort and soundstage.

3) Battery & Charging

Target 20+ hours of battery life for wireless. USB-C is convenient and widely supported.

4) Mic & Sound Quality

Look for clear mids for voices and balanced bass. If you take calls often, prioritize dual-mic or ENC.

5) Shortlist & Compare

List three options in your budget and note pros/cons. Choose the one that matches your top two priorities.
